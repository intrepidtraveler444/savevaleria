Her story

A bright future, interrupted in an instant

Valeria had just turned 18. Like so many young people on the edge of adulthood, she was full of plans — chief among them, a dream of becoming a veterinarian and caring for animals.

On 3 July, everything changed. While travelling in San Miguel de Allende, Mexico, another vehicle veered her off the road. The crash left her with devastating injuries: a brain contusion, a jaw fractured in multiple places, and a punctured lung.

Today, Valeria lies in a coma in the intensive care unit, fighting for her life. Her family is by her side — and now they are asking for help to keep her there, and to give her the chance to recover and chase the future she was working so hard for.

The need

Why help is needed right now

Intensive care is relentless — and relentlessly expensive. Every day Valeria spends in the ICU adds to a bill her family cannot carry alone.
